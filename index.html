about me

Sadie Smeenk is a Technical Communication student at Iowa State University. She was homeschooled through high school and took classes at DMACC and ISU during her junior and senior years, placing multiple times on the president’s list for academic excellence while also working three jobs in customer service, communication, and education.

Writing is one of Sadie’s greatest passions, and she believes that effective communication is the key to success in any business. Whether that communication be spoken or written, internal or external, communicators who reflect the values of their organizations can build and maintain healthy relationships with investors, employees, and customers. After graduation, Sadie hopes to harness her experience in content creation and publicity to aid small businesses in developing customer relationships.
