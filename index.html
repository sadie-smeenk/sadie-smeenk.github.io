Who I Am

I’m a writer, a reader, a hiker, a baker, a lover of Greek tragedy, a collector of socks, a sister to two, an Iowa native, and a lifelong student.

Why Communication

Writing is one of my greatest passions, and I believe that effective communication is the key to success in any business. Whether that communication be spoken or written, internal or external, communicators who reflect the values of their organizations can build and maintain healthy relationships with investors, employees, and customers.

What I Can Do

While my degree is in technical communication, that doesn’t encompass everything I’ve done as a student and a professional. Through my classes, I’ve developed skills in all kinds of communication, from web development to video production to public relations. In the workplace, I’ve led social media campaigns, written newsletters for thousands of readers, planned dozens of events, and learned new things on the job every single day. I’m ready to tackle my next challenge!
